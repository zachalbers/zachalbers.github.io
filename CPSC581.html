Project 0: Homer Simpson

A 'button' that was developed based on the characteristics and iconic traits of Homer Simpson. Built with JavaScript.

Project 0: Homer Simpson


Initial Sketches:

Project 0 started with every group member sketching 10 different ideas, from which we would have a large selection to move forward with. I started by interviewing some of my classmates to generate some initial sketches and also experimented with some celebrity ideas.




Further Sketching and Iteration:

Once each team member had generated lots of ideas, we came together to choose an idea to go forward with. Some interesting sketches had been done on Homer Simpson, so we decided to pursue that character and sketch some ideas further off of it.







Implementation:


After everyone created additional sketches, we met together and brainstormed additional ideas and came to a consensus on what we were going to create. We wanted to represent Homer without showing him in the scene, so we decided to depict him in two different iconic scenarios, which would clue user quickly as to whom the character is. There a different interaction in each scene, the donut and the beer, that trigger animations and phrases from Homer.
