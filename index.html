Summary

The focus of this project was to develop a prototype website for a custom brewery delivery service, using research and applicable design techniques. Over the course of this project a team three students and I researched the market segment and brainstormed ideas for a web-based solution. We followed a design cycle of sketching, more brainstorming and iteration, building basic wireframes of the website in Balsamic, more brainstorming and iteration, then building a high-fidelity prototype in Adobe XD.

  • Brainstorming and sketching initial designs
  • Researching
